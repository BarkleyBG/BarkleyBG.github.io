About me

I'm working on my PhD in Biostatistics at UNC-Chapel Hill.

My main area of study is causal inference in observational studies, especially with infectious diseases. I'm interested in adapting new machine learning methods for estimation, but it's also important to ask good questions with well-defined causal parameters!

For fun, I put on my data scientist hat. I'm dabbling in some small side projects in:

  • Politics and news journalism
  • Football
  • Sexual assault
  • Exercise habits

I'm looking for Dutch-language statistics blogs and twitter accounts to follow so I can practice my skills. Send them my way! I also speak French. Romanian is on my list for next year.
